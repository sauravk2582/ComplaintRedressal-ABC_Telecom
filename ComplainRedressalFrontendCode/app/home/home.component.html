ABC Telecom stands as India's leading and fully-integrated telecommunications service provider. Our extensive clientele comprises over 40,000 Indian and multinational corporations, ranging from small and medium enterprises to nearly 300 top-tier enterprise and carrier companies worldwide.

ABC Telecom serves 90 percent of the nation's population through a fiber optic cable network spanning over 190,000 kilometers. Additionally, the company possesses substantial tower infrastructure to support other telecommunications operators.

ABC Telecom offers data solutions tailored for households and small businesses in India's major 29 cities, focusing on buildings linked to the Reliance network. Additionally, Reliance Communications, a subsidiary of ABC Telecom, possesses the world's largest private undersea cable system through its entity Global Cloud Xchange. This system provides a wide range of customized solutions for carriers, enterprises, and new media companies on a global scale.

GCX's undersea cable system covers over 67,000 route kilometers and seamlessly integrates with Reliance Communications' 200,000 route kilometers of domestic optic fiber backbone. This integration creates a robust Global Service Delivery Platform for Enterprises. GCX is uniquely positioned to assist businesses by deploying Next-Generation Enterprise solutions across its Cloud Delivery Networks. Since its establishment in December 2002, Reliance Communications has been transforming global communication, influencing not only lifestyles but also the lives of its customers worldwide.
